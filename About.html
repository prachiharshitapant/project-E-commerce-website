Kitty Cat

Kitty Cat brand has been making food for cats since 1958 and we use this experience in everything we do. Not only are we committed to developing delicious products. The brand is there for the whole journey as your cat grows with us, we are constantly looking for ways to improve our nutrition and care. The makers pride themselves on producing food that give cat owners the reassurance they are feeding their cat a healthy and tasty diet. We work with the WALTHAM® Centre for Pet Nutrition to develop products that constantly improve nutrition and care. We ensure all our meals are full of the natural goodness your cat instinctively needs as well as always being nutritionally complete and balanced, helping the world’s cats to live healthier and happier lives.
